## ⚖️ CODE OF CONDUCT

✨ **WELCOME TO THE PARAMPARA COMMUNITY!**

### 📜 Our Pledge

We are dedicated to fostering a collaborative, inclusive, and culturally respectful environment for all contributors. As members and contributors of Parampara, we pledge to make participation a harassment-free and enriching experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

Because our project centers on preserving rural cultural heritage, we hold an extra tier of respect for the traditions, artifacts, and communities (such as the artisans of Kantha, Madhubani, and Dokra) that we document and share.

### 🤝 Our Community Standards

✅ **What We Encourage 👇**

- **Showing Empathy & Respect:** Toward fellow contributors and the cultures we represent.
- **Using Welcoming Language:** Creating an inclusive space for developers and cultural enthusiasts alike.
- **Constructive Feedback:** Helping each other grow technically and contextually.
- **Accepting Responsibility:** Owning mistakes and learning from them gracefully.
- **Respectful Dissent:** Discussing technical or design differences with patience.

❌ **What We Discourage (Zero Tolerance)**

- Harassment, exclusionary jokes, or discrimination of any kind.
- Cultural appropriation or disrespectful commentary regarding the rural traditions and stories we archive.
- Confidentiality breaches (especially concerning sensitive location or contact data for local hosts).
- Trolling, disparaging comments, or derogatory remarks.
- Any act that makes others uncomfortable or unsafe.

### 🛡️ Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior to ensure a safe and productive environment. We commit to taking appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any instances of unacceptable behavior, while maintaining the utmost confidentiality to protect those who report incidents.

Maintainers pledge to lead by example—using inclusive language, providing timely and respectful feedback, and prioritizing the project’s mission over personal status. We will not tolerate the abuse of authority, silencing of valid technical dissent, or favoritism.

### 💡 Key Elements Included:

- **Active Duty:** Monitoring the community and responding to reports.
- **Ethical Boundary:** Protecting the privacy of rural communities and avoiding bias.
- **Leadership Role:** Setting a respectful tone for cultural digital archiving.

### ❤️ Our Commitment

We are dedicated to fostering a culture of mutual respect and continuous learning. By contributing to this project, we pledge to uphold these values and hold ourselves accountable to the community and the heritages we aim to protect.

Thank you for making Parampara a safe space for preserving history!

✨ _End of Code of Conduct._
